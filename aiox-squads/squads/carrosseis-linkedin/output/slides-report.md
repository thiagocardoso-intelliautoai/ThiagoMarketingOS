## Design System

| Token | Valor | Rationale |
|-------|-------|-----------|
| Background | #F8F8F8 | Off-white editorial, neutro e premium |
| Text Primary | #1A1A1A | Quase preto, contraste 15.3:1 com background |
| Text Secondary | #444444 | Cinza escuro para body, contraste 8.1:1 |
